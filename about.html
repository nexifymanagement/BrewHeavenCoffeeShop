Our Story

Founded in 2010 by two coffee enthusiasts, Brew Haven began as a small corner shop with a big dream - to serve exceptional coffee in a warm, welcoming environment. What started as a humble operation with a single espresso machine has grown into a beloved community hub.

We're proud to have maintained our commitment to quality while expanding our offerings. Every bean we serve is ethically sourced from small farms we've personally visited across Central and South America, Africa, and Southeast Asia.

Our roasting facility, added in 2015, allows us to control every aspect of the flavor profile, ensuring each cup meets our exacting standards. We still use the same traditional roasting methods we started with, just on a slightly larger scale!
