Frequently Asked Questions

Do you take reservations?

We operate on a first-come, first-served basis and don't take reservations for regular seating. However, for groups of 8 or more, please contact us in advance and we'll do our best to accommodate you.

Do you offer catering services?

Yes! We provide full coffee catering services for events, meetings, and special occasions. Our catering menu includes a variety of coffee options, pastries, and light bites. Contact us for more details and pricing.

Are you hiring?

We're always looking for passionate coffee enthusiasts to join our team. Please send us your resume and a brief cover letter explaining why you'd like to work at Brew Haven. No experience is necessary - we provide full training!

Do you sell your coffee beans?

Absolutely! We sell all of our house-roasted beans by the bag in-store. We also offer nationwide shipping through our online store (coming soon). Ask our baristas about our current selection and tasting notes.

Is your space available for private events?

Yes, we host private events after hours. The space can accommodate up to 50 people. We offer various packages that include coffee service, food options, and staffing. Contact us for availability and pricing.
